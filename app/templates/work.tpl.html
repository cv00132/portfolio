Recent Projects

Barkspark

BarkSpark was designed with dog lovers in mind. The idea arose from the lack of sites that connected dog owners to other dog owners. I love spending time with my hound and wanted a way to connect with others looking to meet with while spending time with their fur friends.

General Knowledge

General Knowledge is a military themed trivia game built using object oriented programming. Players choose a random category and then have 15sec to answer the question. Selected questions go away after they're chosen, so choose wisely!

Prost!

Prost! is a responsive website designed for a German bierhaus. It uses AJAX to populate the menu, flickr API to pull in the photos, and Google Maps API to generate a localized map of the area. It was built in collaboration with a partner.
