Recent Projects

Barkspark

BarkSpark was designed with dog lovers in mind. The idea arose from the lack of sites that connected dog owners to other dog owners. I love spending time with my hound and wanted a way to connect with others looking to meet with while spending time with their fur friends.

General Knowledge

General Knowledge is a military themed, object-oriented trivia game. Players choose a random category and then have 15sec to answer the question. Selected questions go away after they're chosen, so choose wisely!

Prost!

Prost! was one of my first big projects, as well as, the first time I worked with a partner. I learned a lot going through the design process and I realized that compromise is key when deciding which features to add, styles to use, and how all of it goes together.

Vaughan Songs

Vaughan's Songs was a simple app created using Soundcloud's API. It was the first time I was given freedom to create a project with no boilerplate. Even though I only had a few days and I overhauled the design multiple time, I'm still very happy with how it turned out.
