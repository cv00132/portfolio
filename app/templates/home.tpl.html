About Me

After graduating Georgia Southern University, I began my career working for a startup REIT (real estate investment trust) where I spent most of my time writing ad copy, taking photographs, and collecting data for marketing meetings. This lead me into the world of freelancing where I spent the next year as a real estate photographer. I began my journey into programming shortly after, and with the help of my old college textbooks and online courses I was getting up to speed. I enjoyed the thrill of solving problems through code so I decided to take the next step in my development. I completed Codecademy's Ready program, which was a 6-week online bootcamp, that taught me more of the basics, but I needed more "real world" experience. I then enrolled in The Iron Yard's 12-week Front End Development program. I was exposed to the world of web development and engineering, and now I'm looking to continue down this exciting path and into the future of web development.
